Evidence Observatory

A proprietary evidence platform for reconciling wearable, route, episodic, movement, transportation, and documentary sources without losing their identity or limits.

The Evidence Observatory exists because a credible mobility record needs more than a chart. It needs source provenance, compatible comparisons, visible samples, preserved missingness, scientific identity, and a consistent path from evidence to public explanation.

Why the platform exists

Keep Context Intact from Source to Review

Wearable and mobility records answer different questions. A route shows functional travel. Overnight recovery supplies longitudinal context. A targeted RRI session adds episodic specificity. An access record documents what happened in a public system.

The platform keeps those sources connected enough to review together while preventing them from being flattened into one overclaimed signal.

Source-linked evidence

Preserve where an observation came from, what it measured, and which interpretation boundary applies.

Within-person comparison

Organize comparable mobility and transportation contexts while retaining samples, dates, units, and missingness.

Reviewer-safe presentation

Publish bounded findings that can be inspected without exposing private raw records or asking a browser to recalculate science.

Evidence source roles

Longitudinal and Episodic Evidence Differ

Longitudinal context

WHOOP

Activity, strain, sleep, and overnight recovery patterns across time. WHOOP recovery HRV/RMSSD is not session HRV.

Functional travel

Strava

Activity, route, distance, duration, speed, and repeated mobility context.

Episodic evidence

Polar H10 and Kubios

Targeted RRI, HRV/RMSSD, ECG, acceleration, and movement evidence where available.

Source reconciliation relates compatible records; it does not make their fields interchangeable or fabricate values that a source did not provide.

What the system does not claim

Boundaries Are Part of the Architecture

The Evidence Observatory does not diagnose, directly measure pain, establish universal causation, prove universal safety, decide legal entitlement, or turn one person's record into a general rule.

It also does not make every source equally authoritative. A wearable estimate, route record, personal account, medical document, agency decision, and scientific interpretation remain different evidence classes.

Canonical declaration

Within-person evidence model. Wearable signals estimate physiologic, musculoskeletal, movement, and transport burden; source artifacts and validation layers remain available for audit.
