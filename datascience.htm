Two-Entity Strategy: .com and .org

HandicapSkater.com is the commercial AI/ML wearable data science platform concept: wearable biometrics, HR/HRV/activity evidence, mobility accommodation analytics, and pattern detection for non-standard mobility. It may be a strategic fit, commercial rights package, licensing candidate, or acquisition candidate for health AI, consumer wearable analytics, health data interoperability, accessibility, and wearable data science teams.

HandicapSkater.org is the separate nonprofit standards, civil rights, and accommodation framework: non-traditional mobility aid standards, evidence-based accommodation standards, public-sector accessibility education, supportive wearable documentation, disability rights, and fair access.
