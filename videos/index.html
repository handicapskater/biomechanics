Videos and Public Articles

Movement becomes easier to understand when posture, control, route, transportation, transfer, and access interaction can be seen in context.

The collection documents skates used as a non-traditional mobility aid in public life. Each card identifies its source, context, category, summary status, and related page so future records can be added consistently.

How to use the collection

Video Supplies Functional Context

Footage can show active controlled skating, pain-limited transitions, passive passenger transport, boarding, route conditions, and the practical effect of an access decision. It belongs beside the source-linked movement, transportation, route, and documentary record.
