Upon his first day of employment at a subsidiary of IBM, he realized that it was not possible for him to walk between meetings, because the distances were too great. He requested permission to use his skates as a mobility aid and was granted permission within hours. The second day of work, he brought a spare pair of skates and left them at work to use as a common wheelchair. There were no problems from his use or his ability to travel between meetings.
